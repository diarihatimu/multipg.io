Cafes in Central Singapore

1. The Bravery


With a gorgeous modern interior, you can check out their yummy dishes like the Guac & Eggs with the sourdough made inhouse.

Other yummy dishes also include the Pancakes Stack, Wholesome Breakfast as well as a range of pasta like the Chicken Ragu

and Smoked Duck Aglio Olio. Don't miss out on their signature Lavender Latte, Matcha Latte and their freshly-baked assortment

of brownies and burnt cheesecake too!



The updated menu also features heartier additions, apart from usual brunch offerings of pancakes ($14) and cheese toast ($16).

Stand-outs include the Cod en Papillote ($39), which involves cooking the fish in a paper pouch. Sealed within are slices of

crisp fennel and seasonal greens, cooked in a Japanese-inspired ginger-soy broth. A serving of furikake-topped rice completes

this belly-warming dish.

2. Fluff Stack



Fluff Stack serves some of the fluffiest souffle pancakes with an airy texture that will melt in your mouth. Although

the cafe isn't new, they just got halal-certified recently. It's also one of the few places where you can get halal

souffle pancakes in Singapore. Pick from their top favourite dishes like the Mango Passion Fruit Souffle Pancakes,

Oolong Boba Souffle Pancakes, Hazelnut Chocolate Souffle Pancakes and more!





Fluff Stack

Address: 3 Temasek Boulevard #B1-K4, Mall, West Atrium Suntec City, 038983

Opening hours: 11AM-9.45PM, Daily

Telephone: 8764 3166



3. Flavour Fling



As the first café to offer the immensely popular salted egg yolk croissant, this spot has been a trail-blazer in the café

scene since. Take note of its selection of Swiss Raclette Cheese-doused savoury dishes – they are absolutely dripping with

delectably piquant flavours!




Flavour Fling
